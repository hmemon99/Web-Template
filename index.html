Special Events

To build a sense of community, we plan on hosting several events at our original Piscataway High School branch.

The Shark Tank

In November 2014, we plan on hosting a school-wide Shark Tank-style competition, where members and non-members can create teams to pitch their business ideas to the "sharks."

  • Each team will have 3 people, with 10 teams being the maximum
  • The "Sharks" (judges) are the founding members of DYE
  • Grand Prize of $100 to the winning team
Guest Speakers

Each month, we aim to host at least one guest speaker at our Piscataway branch.

  • Live Guest Speakers + Skype/Virtual Guest Speakers
  • Entrepreneurs from several different industries
  • We aim to bring female entrepreneurs in as speakers as well
  • Entrepreneurs whose ethnicity makes them a minority
Product Sell-Off

During December 2014, we plan on hosting a school-wide "sell-off" (sales competition)

  • One item will be chosen as the designated product to be sold
  • Members will form teams of 4
  • The team with the most sales after two weeks will win
